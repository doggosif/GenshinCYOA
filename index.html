Journey Across Teyvat

Welcome to the world of Teyvat traveler, a vast world full of magic and wonder. You arrived here from another world, and it didn't take long for you to make friends, help cities and villages, defend the world from deadly threats, and encounter gods. With the ability to control the seven elements of Teyvat, you have made your mark on this world, and everyone is indebted to you. You've lived here for a while now, and after many years of difficult tasks, you've decided to take a break and explore the world without any worries or responsibilities.
