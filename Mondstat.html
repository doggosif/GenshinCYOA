Mondstat

Your journey begins in Mondstat, the City of Wind. As the acting grandmaster of the Knights of Favonious, Jean, famously says; "The wind will carry Dandelion Seeds, songs, and stories far afield, and it also guides gentle travelers to it's realm." You've stayed a few nights at Angel's Share Tavern, enjoying the local food and drink the region has to offer, especially the world famous wines produced at the Dawn Winery. Who knows, one day you could visit the vinyards there yourself. You take a few shots with the cavalry leader, Kaeya, visit Lisa at the Grand Library, and listen to one of Barbara's performances at the Favonious Cathedral. Time flies when you're having fun, but it was time for you to head out. You pass the large gate to leave the city, and cross the bridge. Two paths are in front of you. To the left, you see a vast, grassy meadow. To the right, you see the path leads to a small forest, with some small houses peering behind the trees. Which path do you take?
