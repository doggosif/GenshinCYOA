Wangshu Inn

The path ahead of you is strikingly clear, as the big tree house in the middle of an estuary is a landmark you cannot miss. As you trek across the marshy lands, you finally arrive, and realize that this is the world famous Wangshu Inn. The innkeeper recognizes you for your heroic deeds of the past, and offers you the best room on the house. You accept and take a needed rest. In the restaurant, you meet a familiar face. It's Tartaglia, the 11th Harbinger from Snezhnaya, and your old rival. You two catch up over dinner, and he offers to accompany you on the path to Liyue Harbor. You think on that idea, and go to sleep for the night. Which path will you take the next morning? To the left leads to Liyue Harbor, and to the right is more unfamiliar lands.
